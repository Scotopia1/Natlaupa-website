## ARTICLE 3: SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 3.1 Website Development & Maintenance

The Provider shall develop, deploy, and maintain a luxury hotel booking platform with the following features:

**Technical Specifications:**
- Next.js 15 framework with App Router
- React 19 for user interface components
- TailwindCSS for responsive styling
- GSAP and Framer Motion for premium animations
- Lenis for smooth scrolling experience
- Mapbox GL for interactive destination maps
- Google Gemini API integration for AI-powered recommendations

**Website Features:**
- Homepage with hero section, trending hotels, and mood-based matching
- Hotel listing and detail pages with galleries
- Offer listing and detail pages
- Blog section with individual post pages
- Country and style-based hotel filtering
- About and Contact pages
- Hotel partnership application page
- Angel program registration page
- AI-powered concierge recommendations
- GDPR-compliant cookie consent system
- Mobile-responsive design (Desktop, Laptop, Tablet, Mobile viewports)

**API Endpoints (20 endpoints):**
- Hotels management (list, detail, trending, countries)
- Offers management (list, detail)
- Blog posts (list, detail by slug)
- Destinations and Styles
- Contact form submissions
- Hotel inquiries
- Partnership applications
- Angel program applications
- Newsletter subscriptions
- AI recommendations
- User tracking and consent

### 3.2 Administrative Dashboard

The Provider shall develop a comprehensive admin dashboard for business management:

**Content Management:**
- Hotels: Create, read, update, delete hotel listings
- Offers: Manage special offers and promotions
- Blog Posts: Content creation with rich text editing
- Destinations: Manage travel destinations
- Styles/Categories: Manage hotel classification

**Business Management:**
- Inbox for user inquiries with status tracking
- Contact form submissions management
- Partnership applications review and processing
- Angel program applications management
- Newsletter subscriber management with export capabilities
- Analytics dashboard with key performance metrics

**Dashboard Features:**
- Secure authentication and access control
- Clean, intuitive user interface
- Real-time data synchronization
- Export functionality for reports
- Search and filter capabilities

### 3.3 SEO Services (Comprehensive Package)

The Provider shall deliver ongoing SEO services including:

**On-Page Optimization:**
- Meta titles and descriptions optimization
- Header tag structure (H1, H2, H3)
- Image alt text optimization
- Internal linking strategy

**Technical SEO:**

**Ongoing Services:**

## ARTICLE 13: TERMINATION

### 13.1 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason by providing **thirty (30) days written notice** to the other Party.

### 13.2 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

a) Commits a material breach that is not cured within 15 days of written notice

b) Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases operations

c) Fails to make payment within 30 days of the due date (applicable to Provider's right to terminate)

### 13.3 Effects of Termination

Upon termination or expiration:

a) **Payment:** Client shall pay all fees due for services rendered up to the termination date

b) **Deliverables:** Provider shall deliver all completed Deliverables and work in progress

c) **Transition:** Provider shall provide reasonable assistance for transition to a new provider (up to 10 hours at no additional cost)

d) **Data:** Provider shall return or delete Client data per Client's instructions within 30 days

e) **Licenses:** Licenses granted under Article 7 shall survive termination, provided all fees are paid

f) **Hosting:** Provider shall maintain hosting for 30 days post-termination to allow for migration

### 13.4 Survival

The following provisions survive termination: Articles 7 (IP Rights), 8 (Confidentiality), 9 (Data Protection), 10 (Warranties - for the warranty period), 11 (Limitation of Liability), and 14 (Dispute Resolution).
